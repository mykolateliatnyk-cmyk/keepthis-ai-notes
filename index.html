Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new note?
Click the Keep This icon in your menu bar and select "+ New note", or use the keyboard shortcut ⌘N from the app menu.
How do I delete a note?
Open the Board (⌘B) by clicking the menu bar icon → Board. Hover over a note card and click "Delete". Notes can only be deleted from the Board to prevent accidental deletion.
Where is my data stored?
All your notes are stored locally on your Mac. The data folder is located at: ~/Library/Application Support/Keep This/. Your notes never leave your device unless you use an AI feature.
How do AI features work?
When you tap an AI action (Improve, Shorten, Expand, etc.), the text of your current note is sent securely to the Claude API by Anthropic. The result replaces your note text. You can undo any AI action using the "Restore original" button that appears after each AI action.
What is the Daily Prediction?
Every day, Keep This generates a short personal prediction using AI. It appears as a 🔮 button on your sticky note. Tap it to reveal your prediction for the day. A new prediction is generated each day automatically.
How do I hide the Dock icon?
Open the AI panel (✦ button) on any note → Settings → toggle "Hide from Dock". The app will continue running from the menu bar icon.
💡 Make sure the menu bar icon is visible before hiding the Dock icon, so you can still access the app.
How do I manage my Keep This Pro subscription?
Open System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. There you can view, change, or cancel your Keep This Pro subscription. You can also restore your purchase from the upgrade screen in the app.
The app is not responding to keyboard shortcuts
Click on the sticky note first to make sure it's in focus, then try the shortcut. Some shortcuts (like ⌘N for new note) work from any window.
AI features are not working
Make sure you have an active internet connection. AI features require a connection to our secure proxy server. If the issue persists, please contact us.
How do I reset the Daily Prediction for testing?
This is only relevant for developers. Run in Terminal: defaults delete com.keepthis.app prediction_v1
